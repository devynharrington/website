### Hi, I’m Devyn Harrington.

I’m a Senior VMware Cloud Foundation Consultant at [ClearBridge Technology Group](https://www.clearbridgetech.com), a VMware by Broadcom partner supporting federal and Department of Defense environments.

After nearly a decade in the [United States Marine Corps](https://www.marines.mil) as a Data Systems Chief, I transitioned into enterprise virtualization and cloud infrastructure consulting. Today, I specialize in VMware Cloud Foundation, NSX, and secure architecture modernization initiatives.
